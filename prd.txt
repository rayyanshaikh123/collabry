1. Product Vision
1.1 Vision Statement

To create a single, intelligent, and collaborative study environment where students can learn together, stay focused, and grow skills, powered by AI and built for real-world study behavior.

1.2 Long-Term Goal

Collabry aims to become the default AI study companion for studentsâ€”bridging the gap between:

studying and understanding,

collaboration and productivity,

academics and career-oriented upskilling.

2. Problem Overview
2.1 Current Challenges

Students today face multiple systemic problems:

Learning resources are scattered across multiple platforms.

AI tools exist but are isolated and not collaborative.

Students lack motivation, consistency, and structured planning.

Distractions severely reduce study effectiveness.

Regional language and accessibility gaps exclude many learners.

No direct link between academic learning and skill certification.

2.2 Why Existing Solutions Fail

Note-taking apps are not intelligent.

AI chatbots are not contextual to user material.

Collaboration tools are generic and not study-focused.

Course platforms operate separately from academic learning.

3. Product Solution Overview
3.1 What Collabry Does

Collabry is a web-based, AI-powered collaborative study hub that enables:

AI-assisted understanding of study material

Real-time group collaboration

Focused, distraction-free study sessions

Smart study planning and habit formation

Personalized course recommendations for upskilling

All within one unified platform.

4.1 Primary Users

College students (engineering, science, commerce)

Students preparing for exams (GATE, CAT, UPSC, etc.)

Project-based study groups and hackathon teams

4.2 Secondary Users (Future Scope)

Educators and mentors

Institutions and coaching centers

5. Core Features & Requirements
5.1 AI-Powered Learning Engine

Functional Requirements

Upload PDFs, notes, and documents

AI-generated summaries and explanations

Context-aware Q&A from uploaded material

Quiz and flashcard generation

Mind map generation from notes

Non-Functional Requirements

Responses must be relevant to provided content

Low latency for AI outputs

Structured, readable AI responses

5.2 Collaborative Study Workspace

Functional Requirements

Shared study rooms

Live annotations and highlights

Group chat with AI assistance

Collaborative mind maps

Non-Functional Requirements

Real-time sync with low lag

Permission-based access

Conflict-free updates

5.3 Study Planning & Productivity (StudyBuddy)

Functional Requirements

AI-generated daily/weekly study plans

Task-based breakdown of syllabus

Progress tracking

Adaptive planning when sessions are missed

Non-Functional Requirements

Simple, intuitive UI

Quick plan regeneration

Persistence across sessions

5.4 Focus & Motivation System (FocusMode+)

Functional Requirements

Pomodoro-style focus sessions

Tab-switch detection

Focus lock mode

AI-generated motivation prompts

Daily reflection

Non-Functional Requirements

Non-intrusive nudges

Accurate session tracking

Visual progress indicators

5.5 Skill Development & Course Recommendations (SkillBridge)

Functional Requirements

Detect learning interests from study activity

Fetch relevant online courses

AI-based ranking and explanation

Certification tracking

Non-Functional Requirements

Updated course data

Clear reasoning for recommendations

Minimal friction to explore courses

5.6 Inclusivity & Accessibility

Functional Requirements

Multi-language AI output

Voice-based doubt solving

Text-to-speech summaries

Clean, accessible UI
